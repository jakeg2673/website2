About Evilcorp

Established in 1995, Evil Corp has been operating for nearly three decades, lurking in the shadows of the corporate world. Headquartered in the heart of a sprawling metropolis, Evil Corp operates from a sleek, monolithic skyscraper that dominates the city skyline. However, rumors suggest that the true extent of their operations spans across clandestine locations worldwide.

About Evilcorp

Evil Corp prides itself on being the epitome of villainy in the corporate realm. With a mission to dominate markets through unethical practices, they engage in a wide array of nefarious activities, including:

  • Corporate Espionage
  • Monopoly Expansion
  • Environmental Exploitation
  • Labor Exploitation

Evil Corp was founded by the enigmatic and Machiavellian visionary, Dr. Alexander Malicious. A master manipulator and ruthless strategist, Dr. Malicious built the company from the ground up, leveraging his vast wealth and influence to shape Evil Corp into a formidable force in the corporate underworld.

At Evil Corp, a culture of ruthlessness, greed, and deceit permeates every facet of the organization. Employees are encouraged to prioritize self-interest and cutthroat tactics in pursuit of corporate objectives, with loyalty to the company rewarded above all else.

Despite their malevolent activities, Evil Corp maintains a carefully curated public image, presenting themselves as a beacon of innovation and progress. Through strategic PR campaigns and generous donations to political candidates, they seek to whitewash their reputation and evade scrutiny from regulators and the public alike.
